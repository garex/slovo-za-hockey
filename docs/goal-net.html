Structure in which goals are scored. Rectangular in shape; the front frame of the goal is made of steel tube painted red and consists of two vertical goalposts and a horizontal crossbar. A net is attached to the back of the frame to catch pucks that enter the goal and also to prevent pucks from entering it from behind. The entire goal net is considered an inbounds area of the playing surface, and it is legal to play the puck behind the goal net.
